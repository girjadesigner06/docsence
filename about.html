about us

What we do

At Docsense, we provide an AI-driven platform that automates document processing and data extraction workflows, helping businesses streamline operations and eliminate manual tasks.

Using advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and deep learning models, Docsense automates key processes such as accounts payable, order processing, and insurance underwriting. Our platform handles a wide range of unstructured documents — including invoices, receipts, purchase orders, contracts, claims, and forms —converting them into structured, actionable data.

Trusted by 34% of Fortune 500 companies, Docsense scales from small businesses to enterprise-level document automation. Our intuitive, customizable solution seamlessly integrates with your existing systems through APIs, reducing manual effort by up to 90% and delivering accuracy, speed, and cost savings of up to 50%.

Our Visions

Our Vision

Make Machine Learning Ubiquitous and Effortless

We envision a future where machine learning is as foundational and accessible to businesses as databases are today. Our goal is to eliminate the technical barriers that prevent companies from adopting AI, enabling seamless integration of intelligent automation into everyday operations.

Empower Businesses Through Intelligent Automation

We aim to empower organizations—regardless of size or industry—to automate manual, repetitive workflows with the help of advanced AI. By extracting insights from unstructured data, we transform routine operations into efficient, streamlined processes.

Bridge the Gap Between Humans and Data

We believe that AI should work hand-in-hand with people, not replace them. Our vision is to create systems that augment human decision-making, reduce manual effort, and free up time for more strategic, creative work.
